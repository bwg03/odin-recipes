Avocado Toast

Description

This simple yet satisfying avocado toast recipe pairs creamy mashed avocado with protein-packed hard-boiled eggs for a balanced, nutrient-rich breakfast or light lunch. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal prep time, it's a perfect go-to for busy mornings or post-workout refueling.

The buttery avocado spread is seasoned with lemon juice, salt, and pepper, then layered onto crisp toasted bread. Topped with slices of perfectly cooked hard-boiled eggs, this dish delivers a mix of textures and flavors—rich, fresh, and lightly savory. Customize it further with chili flakes, microgreens, or a drizzle of olive oil if you like.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (sourdough, whole grain, or your favorite)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
(Optional toppings: chili flakes, olive oil, micogreens, everything bagle seasoning)

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a pot and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 9–10 minutes.
  2. While eggs cook, toast the 2 slices of bread until golden and crisp.
  3. Once eggs are done, transfer them to an ice water bath for a few minutes to cool, then peel and slice.
  4. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a small bowl.
  5. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the avocado. Mash with a fork until smooth but slightly chunky.
  6. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the two slices of toast.
  7. Top each slice with sliced hard-boiled egg.
  8. Add extra toppings like chili flakes or olive oil if desired, and serve immediately.
