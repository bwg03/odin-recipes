Peanut Butter Banana "Power" Toast

Description

This is the kind of breakfast you make when you want to feel like a fitness influencer, but you also don’t want to actually cook anything. It's just peanut butter. And a banana. On toast. But if you slice that banana just right and maybe sprinkle some cinnamon, you’re basically an aesthetic meal prep god.

It’s quick, filling, protein-rich, and gives off I’m doing my best energy — and honestly, that’s enough.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter (or almond butter if you’re fancy)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • A sprinkle of cinnamon
(Optional toppings: chia seeds, honey, cocoa nibs)

Steps

  1. Toast your bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon of peanut butter on each slice.
  3. Slice banana into coins and layer them neatly (or chaotically) on top.
  4. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Add honey or other toppings if you’re feelin’ it.
  5. Consume while gazing out the window like a character in a coming-of-age film.
